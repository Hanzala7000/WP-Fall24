Full Body Blast Workouts

Workout 01

  • Push-Ups 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bench Press (or Dumbbell Press)3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Tricep Dips (assisted if needed) 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Extensions 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lat Pulldown (or Pull-Ups) 3 sets of 10 reps

Workout 02

  • Seated Rows 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Curls 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Hammer Curls 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Lunges 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Squats (bodyweight or with dumbbells) 4 sets of 10-12 reps

Workout 03

  • Leg Press (or alternative leg machine) 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Calf Raises 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Overhead Shoulder Press (dumbbells or machine) 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lateral Raises 3 sets of 12 reps

Workout 04

  • Front Raises 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Face Pulls (cable or bands) 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank 3 sets of 30-45 seconds
  • Russian Twists 3 sets of 20 reps (10 each side)

Workout 05

  • Leg Raises 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bicycle Crunches 3 sets of 15 reps per side
  • Cardio (optional) 20-30 minutes of moderate-intensity (e.g., treadmill, cycling)
